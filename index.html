Lawful Reductions

i.
Social Security Fund

Enrolling in the SSF waives the 1% social security surcharge applied to the first slab of your income.

ii.
Life Insurance Premiums

Deduct up to NPR 25,000 of premiums paid on a qualifying life insurance policy.

iii.
Health Insurance Premiums

A further deduction of up to NPR 20,000 is permitted against health insurance premiums.

iv.
Pension Income

An additional 25% deduction applies to the first slab for receipts of pension income.

Strategies of Planning

v.
Remote Work Allowance

Up to NPR 50,000 may be claimed where employment is rendered in designated remote areas.

vi.
Filing Jointly as a Couple

Couples enjoy a 0% slab of NPR 600,000 — one lakh higher than the individual filer.

vii.
Approved Retirement Schemes

Contributions to recognised retirement funds carry favourable tax treatment under the Act.

viii.
Income Splitting

Where lawful, distribute earnings across family members to lower the marginal rate borne by any one.
