Tax Distribution by Slab

Tax Reduction Insights

  • Contribute to SSF: Waive the 1% Social Security Tax by joining the Social Security Fund.
  • Life Insurance: Deduct up to NPR 25,000 by paying life insurance premiums.
  • Health Insurance: Deduct up to NPR 20,000 with health insurance premiums.
  • Pension Income: Get an additional 25% deduction on the first slab if you have pension income.
  • Remote Work Allowance: Claim up to NPR 50,000 deduction if working in remote areas.
  • Spread Income: If married, file as a couple to leverage higher initial slabs (NPR 600,000 vs. 500,000).
